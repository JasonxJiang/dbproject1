Dance Dance Revolution at UCLA

The Premier (only) Rhythm Game Club

Mission

To promote rhythm gaming awareness at UCLA. With the advent of realistic dancing games such as Just Dance, people seem to have forgotton about the original rhythm dancing game: Dance Dance Revolution (DDR). We wish to showcase the joys that come with playing the first mainstream rhythm game and help players of all levels improve their skills.

Schedule

The DDR club meets every Wednesday at 8pm come meet us sometime!
